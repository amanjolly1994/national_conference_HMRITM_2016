About HMRITM

HMR Institute of Technology & Managementis located in Hamidpur, New Delhi, India offering both Engineering & Management courses.HMR Institute of Technology & Management offers several courses in the field of engineering at the undergraduate level as well as a postgraduate course in management (MBA), and computer applications(MCA). It is affiliated to the GGSIPU, Delhi. HMR was established in 2002 with the approval of AICTE, Government of NCT, Delhi and the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi.It was opened by the HiraLal, Mohan Devi,Rita Gupta Memorial Trust.The trust has made all efforts to create state of the art educational facilities such as well designed & ventilated class rooms, laboratories, computer labs and library. The infra structural facilities not only provide for existing strength of students in five engineering disciplines but also for future expansions and addition of courses in the field of Technology and Management.

Campus

The campus of the institute is spread over an area of 5 acres offering a safe and secure environment with a wi-fi campus along with cctv surveillance. The campus has a main Administrative Block,Library,Laboratories with latest equipments, workshops, auditorium, conference rooms and a modern canteen.

Academics

The institute is conducting PG programmes in MBA & MCA and B.Tech programmes with strong emphasis on scientific and technological education and research. Recently the institute has tied up with many foreign universities such as Technical University of Sofia and Aalborg University etc to offer summer internship in various fields of technology. The institute also has started the Value Addition Program(VAP) and Project Based Learning(PBL) under which student get to learn about new technology in each new semester and make a project using the respective technology, to show their innovative mind and creativity in the field of technology. HMRITM has the following Engineering Divisions:

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Electrical and ElectronicsEngineering (EEE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical and Automation Engineering (MAE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
