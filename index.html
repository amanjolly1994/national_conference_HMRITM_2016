About The Conference

The First National Conference in "Computational Intelligence, Communication Network, and Smart Grid"(NCCCS-2016) focuses on Advanced Research in the area of Computer Science & Technology, Electronics engineering, Electrical Engineering & it will provide a forum for sharing insights, experiences and interaction on various facets of evolving Technologies and patterns related to Computer Science & Technology, Electronics Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. The main objective of the conference is to help research scholar to focus on their research work for improving the same and making them more relevant and meaningful, through collective efforts. Conference encourages students and researchers to come with innovative ideas on National platform. NCCCS-2016 promotes professional interaction and lifelong learning. .

Conference Objective

Conference aim is to provide a world class platform to researchers to share the research findings by organizing National Conferences. Use the research output of the conference in the class room for their benefits of students/Academicians/Research. Encourage researchers to identify significant research issues in identified areas, in the field of Science, Engineering, and Technology. Conference helps to dissemination of their work through publications in a journal or in the form of conference proceedings or books. The main objective is to help research scholar to focus on their research work for improving the same and making them more relevant and meaningful, through collective efforts. Conference encourages students and researchers to come with innovative ideas on national platform. NCCCS-2016 promotes professional interaction and lifelong learning. Objective of conference is to establish, maintain and manage centers of excellence for the study of related subjects and discipline.
